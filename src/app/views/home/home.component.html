The Idea

About the campaign?

Hello World! All we've ever wished for was "a better place to live in". But have you ever wondered about the magic our body and thought could perform to transform this world into one? If not, then now is the time!


Our country is gifted with incredibly beautiful landscapes and culture. A great number of tourists accross the world choose to experience this grandeur allured by the color and scent of our nation. But are we able to do justice to our guests as well as to ourselves in terms of preserving this spirit?


Unscientific waste management and careless treatment of our ecosystem has led to a rising concern regarding the health and wellness of different species. Human act of throwing litters and damaging the environment are expected to have devastating effects on the lives of next generation. Hence, this very moment counts!

Our Vision

Earth Before Everything Else brings to you an opportunity to be a part of the change. Our initiative of 'Plogging' which inspires our members to pick litters up while jogging guarantees a positive outlook towards the need for conserving and cleaning our environment. We aim to spread awareness among masses and ignite their desire to be the change. Along with a healthy life, this campaign ensures a healthy atmosphere, encouraging the coming generation to carry forward this practice.


Join us, for plogging is the sport of today!
